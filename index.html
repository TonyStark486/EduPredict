EduPredict - Student Dropout Prediction System

A Government Initiative to Reduce Student Dropout Rates Using Data Analytics & AI

About the Project

EduPredict is an AI-powered solution developed under Smart India Hackathon (SIH) to identify and support students at risk of dropping out. The system leverages data-driven insights to enable early intervention and improve academic outcomes.
